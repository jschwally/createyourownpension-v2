Now Let's Look at the Solution

A Fixed Indexed Annuity with a Guaranteed Lifetime Withdrawal Benefit rider is designed to do one specific thing: create a stream of income you cannot outlive, regardless of how long you live or what markets do.

The calculator below lets you explore how a specific premium amount could translate into guaranteed monthly income. You control the inputs. You see the results in real time.

You do not have to close your entire gap with one strategy. Many clients choose to cover a portion of their shortfall with guaranteed income and leave the remainder to their investment portfolio. That balance is personal and we will work through it together.
