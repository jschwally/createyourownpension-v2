What to Expect

This is a free 60 minute Retirement Income Analysis call focused entirely on your situation. No pressure, no obligation, and no sales pitch. We talk through where you are today, where you want to be in retirement, and what gaps exist between the two.

My only goal is to help you find the right solution for your retirement income needs, not mine. I am not committed to any one carrier or product. I work with approximately 40 carriers and recommend only what fits your specific situation and goals. You make the decision. I provide the information and guidance to help you make it with confidence.

How to Prepare

The call works best when you come with a general sense of two numbers: your monthly essential expenses and your guaranteed income sources such as Social Security or any pension. If you have already used the Retirement Paycheck Planner calculator, you already have these numbers ready to go.

No pressure. No obligation. If a guaranteed income strategy makes sense for your situation we will talk about next steps. If it does not, I will tell you that too.
