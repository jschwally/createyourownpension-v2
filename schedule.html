What to Expect

Every call is free, no obligation, and focused completely on your situation. We talk through where you are today, where you want to be in retirement, and what gaps exist between the two.

My only goal is to help you find the right solution for your retirement income needs, not mine. I am not committed to any one carrier or product. I work with approximately 40 carriers and recommend only what fits your specific situation and goals. You make the decision. I provide the information and guidance to help you make it with confidence.

Retirement Income Analysis

Our first conversation. We review your current retirement income picture, identify any gap between your essential expenses and guaranteed income sources, and explore whether a guaranteed income strategy makes sense for your situation.

Pension Design Consultation

A working session for clients ready to move from exploring to designing. We review specific carrier and product options, walk through illustrations tailored to your numbers, and map out a strategy to close your income gap.

Quick Follow-Up

A focused call to answer a specific question or clarify something before your next step. Best for existing clients with a targeted question that does not require a full session.

No pressure. No obligation. This call is a conversation, not a sales pitch. If a guaranteed income strategy makes sense for you, we will talk about next steps. If it does not, I will tell you that too.
